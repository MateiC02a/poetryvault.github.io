Dark poems

The darker side of my imagination. Where I explore the grotesque, existential dread, cosmic horror, violence and the many dark aspects my mind sometimes lingers on.

Always remember the saving mantra:
I do not recognize the bodies in the water!
The lake reflects strange, unknown astra
While forest echoes with eerie laughter.

It cannot be! You tell yourself.
As long dead friend floats up in view.
Madness engulfs you, soiling thyself,
That body next to his, you realize, is you!

Urlă cerul în agonie îngrozitoare,
Nori de grăsime, izvor de unsoare curgătoare,
Plouă cu sânge, puroi și carne putregândă,
Se-apropie bestia vidului, flămândă!

Cu gheare sângerii împarte cerul muribund.
Cu ochi înfometați tânjește-ți sufletul, profund.
Realitatea nebuniei soartei te lovește,
Ființa ta și viața-ți ca hrană deservește!

Feel the itchy, scratchy tingle of her tongue,
Adorned in twitchy, limbly growths,
At ends of which your nightmares clung.
She carves within your flesh her blasphemous oaths.

What madness drove you to this fate?
Embraced in carnal sacrilege with such a thing.
You try to brake, alas it is too late,
Her skin outstretched your world engulfing!

Delapidated body, home to a wretched soul.
Sins stitched messily upon your face,
As if sufficient to pay your toll.
Abandon hope you still have grace!

The old man calls, you cannot hide!
Your path bound, before you lay,
So drag your feet, your fate abide,
The old man waits, your life to take away!

Îl țin cu capul adânc în rană
Până cadavrul îl vede hrană.
Mai bine el decât unul din noi,
Blestemați paznici ai acestui strigoi.

Slujim pe zi, ne-ascundem noaptea,
Sperăm să ne cruțe entitatea.
Nu mă abțin să nu gândesc:
Poate că iadul e mai omenesc!

Vile imbeciles fused up together,
A moaning mass of writhing limbs,
Their flesh melted around the tether,
Dragging them to payment for their sins.

Inane drivel drowned out by the machine,
It's gears oiled by fluids putrid.
They serve a purpose in depths unseen,
Of their fowl presence this world to rid!
